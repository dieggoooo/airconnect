The Platform

Aviation's first three-sided marketplace

AirConnect eliminates the friction of flight training logistics. Browse aircraft, find your instructor, and book your next lesson. All from a single, intuitive interface designed for the modern pilot.

01

Student Pilots

Find certified instructors and available aircraft to complete your training. Browse by location, aircraft type, and instructor ratings, then book with a single tap.

02

Flight Instructors

Expand your student base and find aircraft to instruct from. Set your availability, manage your schedule, and let students come to you. All through one streamlined platform.

03

Aircraft Owners

Monetize your aircraft by connecting with vetted pilots and instructors. Approve or deny rental requests, manage your fleet calendar, and generate revenue on your terms.
